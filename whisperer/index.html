Why Whisperer

🔒

Truly private

Every word is transcribed locally. Audio is processed in memory only — never uploaded, never written to disk.

⚡

Instant transcription

A dedicated engine returns text in about 0.2 seconds — with punctuation — far faster than Whisper alone.

🌐

Smart language routing

Each sentence is routed to the best engine automatically, so you get fast, accurate results whatever language you speak.

⌨️

Works everywhere

Dictate into any app with a text field — notes, chat, code, email. Hold right ⌘, speak, release.

🧩

Your choice of models

Pick the accuracy/speed tradeoff that fits your Mac. 8 GB machines get a lightweight model recommended automatically.

🪶

Light footprint

Lives quietly in the menu bar. Around 0.5 GB of memory in use; runs smoothly even on entry-level Apple Silicon.
