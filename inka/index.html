Why Inka

Fast, private, effortless

Truly private

Every word is transcribed locally. Audio is processed in memory only — never uploaded, never written to disk.

Instant transcription

A dedicated engine returns text in about 0.2 seconds — with punctuation — far faster than Whisper alone.

Smart language routing

Each sentence is routed to the best engine automatically, so you get fast, accurate results whatever you speak.

Works everywhere

Dictate into any app with a text field — notes, chat, code, email. Hold the key, speak, release.

Your choice of models

Pick the accuracy and speed that fit your Mac. Lower-spec machines get a lightweight model recommended automatically.

Light footprint

Lives quietly in the menu bar. Around half a gigabyte of memory in use; smooth even on entry-level Apple Silicon.
