Why Inka

Private, fast, and yours forever

Private by design

Real privacy isn't a setting. Inka simply never connects to the internet, so your voice has nowhere to go. (Curious? Open Activity Monitor and watch it stay silent.)

As fast as you think

Speak and your words are there in about 0.2 seconds, already punctuated, nothing to clean up. It keeps pace with how fast you talk.

Speaks every language you do

Cloud dictation often turns to gibberish the moment you mix languages. Inka catches each one you actually speak, accurately and punctuated: Chinese, English, Japanese and more, even mixed in a single sentence.

Works in every app

Email, chat, notes, docs, code, your browser. Text drops right where your cursor is. Pace the room or hold your coffee: hands-free mode means you never touch the keyboard.

Pay once, keep it forever

A history only you can see

Optionally keep a searchable record of everything you've dictated — text only, stored on your Mac, never your audio. Off by default, and erased the moment you switch it off.

How it works

1

Install & grant access

Drag Inka to Applications, then allow Microphone and Accessibility when prompted.

2

Download the voice engine

One click in the setup. Everything stays on your Mac.

3

Dictate anywhere

Put your cursor in any text field, hold your trigger key (right ⌘ by default, or ⌥ / ⌃ / fn), speak, and release. Tap to toggle hands-free; press Esc to cancel.

Questions

Good to know

Which languages does Inka support?

Many, including English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and more. You can mix them inside a single sentence.

Is it really offline? How can I trust that?

Everything runs on your Mac, and Inka simply never connects to the internet, so your voice can't go anywhere. Want proof? Open Activity Monitor and watch: it never makes a single connection.

Do I have to pay again for updates?

No. Every license includes lifetime updates. Buy once and keep getting improvements.

Got a new Mac? Can I move my license?

Yes. Each tier covers a set number of devices (1 / 3 / 5). Deactivate on an old machine and activate on the new one.

Does the free trial need an account or credit card?

Neither. The 14-day trial runs fully offline with no sign-up. Enter a license key whenever you decide to buy.

What are the system requirements?

Apple Silicon (M1 or later) and macOS 14 or newer.

What's your refund policy?

Not happy with Inka? Email support@zenoriatech.com within 14 days of purchase for a full refund.
