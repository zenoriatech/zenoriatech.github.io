Technical Details

Our applications use local storage on your device for all data persistence. All information is contained within your device's secure container and included in your device backups. Except for optional premium features available through the Apple App Store, no network connections are made.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, health data, or usage analytics. Our applications are designed to function entirely offline.

Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device encryption and access controls.
